// About

Builder by necessity,
developer by choice.

I'm Terrell Todd, a self-taught full-stack developer based in Baltimore, MD. I came up through 15+ years in heavy equipment — DC Fire & EMS, Herc Rentals, Stertil-Koni — learning how things break and how to fix them. That mindset carries into how I build software.

I taught myself to code using AI-assisted tools and shipped real products. Not demos. Not prototypes. Apps people use. I founded Todd Logic LLC to house a growing portfolio across health tech, fintech, automotive, civic tech, and faith-based spaces.

ASE certified. OSHA 10-hour certified. Pursuing ALI certification. Faith and community drive everything I build — technology should serve people, not just profit.
